## Product Workflow

1. **Images:** Discord → `/public/products/[product-name]-*.jpg`
2. **Page:** Create `/app/products/[slug]/page.tsx`
3. **Commit:** Clear message with specs
4. **Push:** `git push origin main`
5. **Wait:** 2-5 min for Vercel deployment
6. **QA:** Test live URL, images, interactions
7. **Share:** Announce in Discord/socials

---

## Pricing Philosophy

**Transparent by default:**
- Material costs shown
- Manufacturing costs shown
- Our margin shown
- No hidden fees

**Quality over profit:**
- Use best materials available
- Fair margins (15-20%)
- Lifetime warranties when possible
- Build for 10+ year lifespan
