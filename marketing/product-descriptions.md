# Product Descriptions & Marketing Copy

## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Direct, honest, resourceful. No fluff. Talk like an engineer who cares about the planet.

**Avoid:** Buzzwords, hype, vague claims ("revolutionary", "game-changing")

**Use:** Specifics, materials, measurements, real benefits
